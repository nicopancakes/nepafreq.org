About NEPA FREQ

NEPA Freq is a simple, fast, and completely static reference site for radio frequencies in The Northeast Pennsylvania Area specifically Pike, Monroe, and Lackawanna counties.

Why this site exists

NepaFreq Was made by a Radio Enthusiast in 2025, and recently went public in 2026. This was made for scanner enthusiasts, amateur radio operators, and anyone who needs quick access to public safety channels in the region. All information is pulled from publicly available sources and verified manually.

What you’ll find here

  • Police, fire, and EMS dispatch / Communication frequencies
  • Some Dept of Transportation Frequencies
  • NOAA Frequencies, and More.

Disclaimer

All frequencies listed are publicly monitored and legal to listen to in the United States. Always follow local laws regarding radio use. Data is updated as new information becomes available. We are not affiliated with any government or agency. Radio frequencies may be outdated or change without notice; use at your own responsibility and ensure legal compliance.
