About NEPA Frequencies

A community project for radio enthusiasts in Northeast Pennsylvania

NEPA Freq is a simple completely static reference site for radio frequencies in Northeast Pennsylvania, specifically Pike, Monroe, Lackawanna, and Wayne counties.

Why This Site Exists

Created in 2025 by a local radio enthusiast and made public in 2026, NEPA Freq was built for scanner users, ham radio operators, and anyone who needs quick access to public safety and amateur radio frequencies in the region.

All data is collected from publicly available sources and manually checked & verified.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Police, Fire, and EMS dispatch & operational frequencies
  • Department of Transportation channels
  • NOAA Weather Radio frequencies
  • Other public safety and amateur radio channels

Disclaimer

All frequencies listed are from public sources and are legal to listen to in the United States. This site is not affiliated with any government agency, police, fire, EMS, or private company.

Frequencies can change or become inactive without notice. Use this information at your own risk and always ensure compliance with local, state, and federal laws.
